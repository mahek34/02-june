1-What are the three main layers of software architecture? Write one line about each.
1. Presentation Layer (UI Layer) – Handles user interaction and displays information to the user.
2. Business Logic Layer (Application Layer) – Processes data and applies the application's rules and logic.
3. Data Layer (Database Layer) – Stores, retrieves, and manages data for the application.

2-Define client and server in your own words with one real-life example.
Client: A client is a device or application that requests information or services from another computer.
Server: A server is a computer that provides information or services to clients when requested.
Real-life example: When you open YouTube on your phone to watch a video, your phone acts as the client, and YouTube's computers act as the server that sends the video to your device.

3-Write 2 differences each between desktop apps, web apps, and mobile apps.
>Desktop Apps
1. Installed directly on a computer or laptop.
2. Can often work without an internet connection.
>Web Apps
1. Run in a web browser and do not require installation.
2. Usually need an internet connection to function.
>Mobile Apps
1. Designed specifically for smartphones and tablets.
2. Can use device features like the camera, GPS, and notifications.
